My Maze Game Description

CONTROLS

The path you choose is controlled by the arrow keys starting in the top left corner.

The goal is to make it to the bottom left as efficiently as possible.

  • UP ARROW KEY:   move up one
  • DOWN ARROW KEY:   move down one
  • LEFT ARROW KEY:   move left one
  • RIGHT ARROW KEY:   move right one

FEATURES

  • Walking over previous steps erases the line!
  • We are counting your moves!
  • Click "New Maze" to get a new maze
  • Click "Restart" to reset progress to beginning
  • Click "Solve" to see the solution
